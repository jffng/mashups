Polarized Congress
=================================

####A view of the ~1600 votes made by the U.S. House of Representatives during the 112th Congress.
 
Using the **Sunlight Foundation Congress API**

<https://sunlightlabs.github.io/congress/>

as well as **D3.js**

<https://github.com/mbostock/d3>

and **JQuery**

<http://jquery.com/>
